## What this is (and what it is not)

This is the **local render backend** for Vivijure: the part that actually does the GPU work
(turning your storyboards and prompts into images and video) on **your own computer**, with
**your own graphics card**. You run it yourself, at home, on hardware you own.

That one fact decides everything about privacy here, so it is worth saying plainly:

- **You run it. We do not.** Skyphusion Labs writes and gives away this software. We do not
  host it, operate it, or run a copy of it for anyone. There is no account, no sign-up, no
  server of ours in the middle.
- **Your data never leaves your box.** Your prompts, images, trained models, and finished
  renders stay on your machine. We never see them, because there is nothing of ours for them
  to travel to. This is not a promise we are asking you to trust; it is just how the software
  is built.
- **You are the operator.** Because it runs on your hardware, you are the one in charge of it,
  and you are responsible for using it in line with the laws and rules where you live. (The
  AGPL gives you the software; it does not give you a pass on the law.)
